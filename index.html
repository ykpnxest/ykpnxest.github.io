NATURE'S NEW SUPER MATERIAL

World's first commercially available microfibrillated cellulose

Improved performance in anti-settling and anti-sedimentation through improved yield stress. Versatility and flexibility in formulations through robustness to shear, pH and temperature. And, improving the sustainability profile.

STABILITY AND STRENGTH TO CORRUGATOR ADHESIVES

Increased corrugator speed with improved bonding

Exilva® brings stability and strength to the corrugating adhesive, enabling more efficient production through improved bonding, less delamination, less waste and flatter boards.

NEXT-GENERATION CORRUGATOR ADHESIVE

This is what you will get from Exilva:

High Yield Stress

High yield stress giving improved stability of water borne or polar solvent systems at low shear

Pronounced Shear Thinning

Extreme shear thinning with very fast viscosity recovery provides benefits during applying of water borne or polar solvent systems

High Surface Area

Very high surface area provides substantial binding benefits and film forming of high strength films
